About Me

  • I am an Associate Professor at the Australian National University.
    • I am also an Adjunct Professor at the Carnegie Mellon University, USA.
    • I am also an Adjunct Professor at the Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand.
  • My main interest is cyber security-oriented programming languages but also type systems, software verification, etc.
  • Check out our capability enabled secure web language called Wyvern developed in collaboration with Carnegie Mellon University.
  • I am interested in anything fun to do with programming languages might work.
  • I am always looking for any graduate students, while I may have funding, there may also be funding available via the ANU PhD scholarships scheme.
  • Feel free to email me or drop by my office to discuss your options.
  • Before you email me with a query about potential PhD project, if you want me to respond, then please READ THIS FIRST.
